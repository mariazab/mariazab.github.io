About me

My name is Marysia (officially Maria, but I prefer to be called like this. It’s a Polish thing). Currently, finishing my Business Information Technology degree at Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences in Helsinki, Finland.

In 2016, I got bachelor’s degree in International Business at University of Economics in Katowice, Poland. Back then, I was feeling that my studies aren’t completely my thing and are not that interesting to me. I wasn’t sure where to go from there, or even if I wanted to continue in this direction. After some fortunate coincidences, I found myself living in Finland, where I stumbled upon the BITE studies at Haaga-Helia. I immediately became interested, seeing an opportunity to change my career path, as well as maybe discover something exciting to do in my life (especially that I always wanted to learn coding anyway). So, I applied. At first, as an open student, as the deadline had passed already, but also to see how I like it. The first semester was great. It turned out that not only I enjoy programming a lot but I’m also good at it. Three years later, I completed the Software Engineering specialisation with flying colours and I’m about to complete my degree. During this time, I gained a lot of knowledge on many various courses and I had fun making individual and team projects 😄
