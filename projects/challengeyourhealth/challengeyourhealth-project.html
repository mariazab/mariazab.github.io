Challenge Your Health

Previous Next

Challenge Your Health is my thesis project. The app is designed to help and motivate users to live a more healthy lifestyle by providing challenges. The users can choose premade challenges, that are based on the research from the thesis. They can also create their own challenges. Based on the challenge type, the users can track their diet, physical activity or water intake. Additionally, the app allows for setting goals, tracking weight and calculating Body Mass Index, Basal Metabolic Rate and Total Energy Consumption.

The app is built with Java and Android Studio. Data is saved on a local SQLite database with the use of Room persistence library. The app is available on Google Play.



Technologies: Java, Android Studio, SQLite, Room persistence library
